Purpose

The platform will serve as a central hub connecting local businesses with national and international trade opportunities. It will provide key information on business regulations, trade events, and networking opportunities

Scenarios

  • How can i register my business with the chamber of commerce?
  • What networking events or business expos are available for local entrepreneurs?
  • Are there any local grants or financial assistance programs for small businesses?
